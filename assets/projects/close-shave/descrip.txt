Description:
A publication documenting a material research into shaving cream. Starting with experiments using shaving cream as a material & tool to add texture to imagery, reflection on the process spiraled it into larger themes. The project tackles the gendered history of shaving, psychoanalytical theory of the skin as a mental and physical barrier, as well as my own anecdotal relation and experience with the material.
