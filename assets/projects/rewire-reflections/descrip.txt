Name: Rewire Reflections

Tags:

Details: 20x28cm

Description:
A publication project from KABK in collaboration with Rewire Festival. A group of students was asked to work on the second iteration of the Rewire Reflections zine. In the project writers are invited to a workshop during which they, before and during the festival, write about performances, interview artists, and reflect on the themes of the festival. We as the designers shared a space with all the writers and were tasked with designing and finishing the zine during the first 3 days of festival. After the third day 200 of the zines were riso printed overnight to host a launch party on the last day in which everyone was invited to help fold the publication, whilest we pulled them through a sewing machine to bind them.
